One Senate Report Changed Everything

"Since March 9, 1933, the United States has been in a state of declared national emergency."

- Senate Report 93-549 (1973)

That's not a conspiracy theory. It's a Senate report. The same report goes on to say:

"This vast range of powers, taken together, confer enough authority to rule the country without reference to normal constitutional processes."

- Senate Report 93-549 (1973)

In 1933, six words were added to the Trading with the Enemy Act of 1917. Six words that changed who the "enemy" was:

"any person within the United States"

You were never taught this in school. But it's right there in the Congressional Record. The book shows you exactly what happened, why it happened, and what God's Word says about how to thrive anyway.
